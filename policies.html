Salon Policies

Please review before booking your appointment

Thank you for choosing us for your hair services. Please read the policies below before booking your appointment.

1. Payments

We accept cash and card payments. Card transactions may include an additional processing fee depending on the booking/payment system used.

2. Rescheduling

Clients may reschedule their appointment at least 48 hours before their scheduled time. Please contact us as soon as possible if you need to move your appointment.

3. Cancellations

If you need to cancel, please do so at least 48 hours before your appointment. This helps us keep the schedule fair and allows another client the opportunity to book.

4. No Call / No Shows

If you miss your appointment without notice, you may be required to contact us before booking again.

5. Late Arrivals

Clients are given a 10-minute grace period. If you arrive more than 10 minutes late, your appointment may need to be rescheduled.

6. Deposits

Deposits are non-refundable and go toward the total cost of your service. Deposits may be required for certain appointments, including longer services, color services, extensions, or new client bookings.

7. Service Changes

Please book the correct service when scheduling. Major changes, such as adding color, highlights, treatments, or extra styling, may not be available at the time of your appointment if enough time was not reserved.

8. Hair Prep

Please arrive with hair properly detangled and ready for service unless your appointment includes a wash, blowout, or prep service. Extra detangling, product buildup removal, or additional prep may result in an extra charge.

9. Refunds and Adjustments

All services are final. If you have a concern with your hair, please contact us within 48 hours so we can discuss an adjustment if needed. Refunds are not guaranteed.

We understand that emergencies happen and will do our best to accommodate unforeseen circumstances. However, these policies are enforced to respect everyone’s time and keep scheduling fair for all clients.

Thank you for your understanding and cooperation.
